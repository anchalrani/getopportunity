- Mining Mate : 11 posts, Pay Scale : Rs. 15830 - 22150
- Blaster : 03 posts, Pay Scale : Rs. 15830 - 22150

**How to Apply** : Apply Online at SAIL website from 13/11/2015 to  12/12/2015 only.
