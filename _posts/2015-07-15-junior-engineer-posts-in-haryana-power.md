- Junior Engineer (Electrical) : 192 posts (HVPNL-166, UHBVNL-26)
- Junior Engineer (Electrical) : 27 posts (HVPNL-20, UHBVNL-7)
- Junior Engineer (Civil) : 69 posts (HVPNL-29, UHBVNL-4, DHBVNL-14, HPGCL-22)
