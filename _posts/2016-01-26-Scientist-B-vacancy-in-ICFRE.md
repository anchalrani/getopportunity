Applications are invited for the following posts of Scientist-B in ICFRE :

- Scientist -B : 08 posts (UR-3, SC-3, ST-2, PH-1) in various disciplines,  Pay Scale : Rs. 15600 - 39100 grade pay Rs. 5400/-, Age : 30 years
