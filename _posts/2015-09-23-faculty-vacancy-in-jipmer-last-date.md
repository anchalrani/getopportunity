Applications are invited by the Director, Jawaharlal Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education and Research, Puducherry - 605006 from eligible candidates for the following Faculty posts :

- Professor  : 23 posts, Pay Scale :  Rs.37400-67000 + GP Rs.10500/-, Age : 58 years
- Assistant Professor : 45 posts, Pay Scale :  Rs.15600-39100 + GP Rs.8000/-, Age : 50 years

**How to Apply** : Apply Online at JIPMER website from up to 21/10/2015. The duly filled in prescribed application forms for the post should reach to the ‘Director, JIPMER, Dhanvantari Nagar, Puducherry-605006’  up to 28/10/2015.

Please visit <http://jipmer.edu.in/announcements/faculty-recruitment-2015>  for details and  online application forms.
