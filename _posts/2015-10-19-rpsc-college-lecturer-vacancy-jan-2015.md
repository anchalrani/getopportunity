Recruitment for Lecturers in College Education Department  

RPSC invites Online application for following Jobs vacancies of  Lecturers in various Government Colleges in the state of Rajasthan :

Lecturers (Vyakhyata- व्याख्याता) : 1070 1207 posts in various subjects in Colleges in Rajasthan, Pay Scale : PB-3 Rs. 15600-39100 Grade Pay Rs. 6000/-, Age : 21-35 years.

**How to Apply** : Apply Online at RPSC website on or before 16/11/2015 12.00 midnight. 

Information : For complete information and  apply online, please visit  at <http://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in>
